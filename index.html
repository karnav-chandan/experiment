Q. Are you a normal freelancer?

No. I am no longer operating as a typical freelancer. My work is outcome-driven, based on ownership, experimentation, and long-term system thinking rather than short-term tasks.

No. This website is an experimental documentation platform created to explain learning, experimentation, and real-world technical experience before scaling to larger projects.
